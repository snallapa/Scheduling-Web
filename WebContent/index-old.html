Scheduling

Edit Schedules

Easily edit schedules in a table format. Simply click on the name of the resident and click on time blocks located on the schedule to add and change classes.

Add and Remove Residents

Quickly and easily remove users by clicking on their name and clicking on the remove button. Also, easily add users just by clicking the "+" located above the search bar.

Search Residents

To easily find residents, a search bar is located above the list of residents. The list shortens automatically as characters are inputed to make finding users easier.
